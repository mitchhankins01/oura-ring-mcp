# Example Outputs

See what the Oura MCP server returns when you ask Claude about your health data.

## Data Tools

### Sleep ([full example](sleep-output.md))
Ask: *"How did I sleep last night?"*

Formatted sleep data with score, timing, stages breakdown, biometrics (HR, HRV, breathing rate, SpO2), and score contributors.

### Readiness ([full example](readiness-output.md))
Ask: *"What's my readiness today?"*

Readiness score with all contributors (sleep balance, HRV, temperature, etc.) and actionable insights.

### Activity ([full example](activity-output.md))
Ask: *"How active was I today?"*

Steps, calories, activity breakdown by intensity, and movement goals.

### Workouts ([full example](workouts-output.md))
Ask: *"What workouts did I do this week?"*

Workout sessions with type, duration, calories, heart rate, and intensity.

## Resources

### Today Summary ([full example](today-output.md))
Ask: *"Give me a quick health overview"*

Complete daily snapshot: sleep, readiness, activity, and stress in one view.

### Weekly Summary ([full example](weekly-summary-output.md))
Ask: *"Give me a summary of my week"*

7-day overview with averages, best/worst days for sleep, readiness, and activity.

## Smart Analysis

### Anomaly Detection ([full example](smart-analysis-output.md#anomaly-detection))
Ask: *"Are there any unusual readings?"*

Finds outliers using statistical analysis and identifies patterns.

### Condition Comparison ([full example](smart-analysis-output.md#compare-conditions))
Ask: *"How does alcohol affect my sleep?"*

Compares your metrics with and without specific tags, with statistical significance.

### Best Sleep Conditions ([full example](smart-analysis-output.md#best-sleep-conditions))
Ask: *"What leads to my best sleep?"*

Identifies what behaviors and conditions predict your best vs worst sleep nights.

### Sleep Quality Analysis ([full example](sleep-quality-output.md))
Ask: *"Analyze my sleep quality over the past month"*

Comprehensive analysis: trends, sleep debt, stage distribution, timing regularity, and day-of-week patterns.

### Period Comparison ([full example](compare-periods-output.md))
Ask: *"Compare my sleep this week vs last week"*

Side-by-side comparison of two time periods across sleep, readiness, and activity metrics.

### HRV Trend ([full example](hrv-trend-output.md))
Ask: *"Is my HRV improving or declining?"*

Rolling averages, trend direction, recovery patterns, and day-of-week analysis.

### Correlations ([full example](correlation-output.md))
Ask: *"Is there a relationship between activity and sleep?"*

Statistical correlation between any two metrics with interpretation and recommendations.
